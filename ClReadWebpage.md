======================================================================================================



[ReadWebpage](ClReadWebpage.htm) reads a webpage and saves it as a text
file.



[ReadWebpage](ClReadWebpage.htm) assumes:

-   Ubuntu/Linux
-   snarf or wget



  ----------------------------------------------
  ` snarf www.richelbilderbeek.nl - > out.txt`
  ----------------------------------------------



  --------------------------------------------
  ` wget www.richelbilderbeek.nl -o out.txt`
  --------------------------------------------
